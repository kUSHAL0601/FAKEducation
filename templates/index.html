How can you use FAKEducation?

You can use FAKEducation to generate deep-fakes from a single image or a short video. The content which needs to be discussed can be provided as a text file or an audio file. It is simple to use, hence making it easier for parents to use it.

Easy to make animations

Animated movies/videos of kid's favourite characters can be easily made using a single image and content.

Turn Podcasts into Educational Lectures!

With the increase in podcast culture over the past few years, it can be really helpful to convert podcasts to video lectures with a short video/image!

Turn text to interactive sessions!

Easily convert reading activities or notes to videos, helping increase the attention span!
